Our Legacy

The Stellar Group is a Real Estate Development company based out of the Delhi NCR Region. Over its past 25+ years, it has established a reputation for building best-in-class projects with a focus on quality and timely delivery. The Group has capabilities across all asset classes, from residential to commercial, and industrial to hospitality projects, having built over 10 mln+ sq ft.

Our Capability

The Group has forward integrated by establishing its own design, architectural, structural and MEP services design firm. Over the past 25+ years it has also built its in-house construction and MEP services execution capability, including establishing a pre-cast concrete manufacturing facility to fabricate and assemble up to 1 mln sq ft of built up area per year.

Our Excellence

The Group has a professional project management division that safeguards quality and safety standards across projects, while investing in R&D of new construction methodologies and materials. The Group has invested heavily and been rewarded across multiple projects by attaining highest quality standards of sustainable project design with "Platinum" rated Green Projects.
